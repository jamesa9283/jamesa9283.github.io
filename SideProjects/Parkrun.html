Parkrun

Parkrun is a weekly timed 5k event held at 9am across the UK and now across the world. I partake most... ok, some saturdays when I am not busy or oversleep. My times are pretty abismal, but its not a race its all just for fun (My times are usually sub 30 though).
So what side project can involve Parkrun? Well people go round parkruns as 'Parkrun Tourists' these people attempt to visit every parkrun through the alphabet. Thats what this page is dedicated to.
