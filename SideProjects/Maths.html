Mathematical Coding

I call myself a mathematical programmer, what is a mathematical progammer?
A mathematical programmer is a person that uses programming to solve mathematical problems, i.e. Mathematica.
What I do is sometimes the other way around but I enjoy the use of my programming skills and my passion for mathematics together.
As with my websites my Raspberry Pi is the workhorse of my mathematical escapades, when a plain fountain pen and paper wont do. I have a couple of projects I am working on at the moment, one a short term project, the other a longer term project, both engrained into mathematics.

I offer up my services for commisions of mathematical occurances or objects. If theres something you need an animation for, I can code it and you can use it, for a small fee. If you need something, please get in touch at: jamesarthur7@icloud.com
